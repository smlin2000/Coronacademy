Get Started

What is Collab?

Collab was designed due to the increased difficulty
of school because of the COVID-19 epidemic. In past times
study sessions, reviews, and meeting with friends for cramming
was an easy task. With the current global pandemic meeting in
person has become a much more deadly prospect. In order to
adapt with the times Collab was designed in order to
allow students to connect with their friends and classmates.

Ratings and Rewards

Collab was designed with the intention of helping students find ways to study with each other. In order to promote this we have a rating system for people that allows them to provide feeback on the quality time they spent with tutors and fellow students. Those who acheive high ratings will receive extra benefits such as private call rooms, unique profile borders, and much more.

How it Works

You start on the sign-up page where you need to create an account. From there the system will redirect you to the home page where your study experience begins!

Home Page

The Home Page has a Navigation bar on top in order to provide quick and easy access to the various avenues for help. Alternatively, you can also use the "Get Started" button. Our aim is to provide the best accessibility to students nation wide who are suffering from the pandemic.

Courses

The courses page has a comprehensive Calendar showing all the avalible study times for the current month. The Calendar will show study times on a subject by subject basis. Clicking on the subjects in the lower tabs will bring you to the respective website where you can join a live chat, voice chat, and latest forum posts to keep you updated with the information that you need for the topic. The Calendar will change as more events will be hosted over the course of the year so keep an eye out for those changes as well. More courses will become available as more tutors come so if you want to be a qualified tutor, try it out!

Subjects

Each subject has its own page with numerous features included. In each subject, you will be presented brief information about the topic. Because our aim is for tutoring and not for videos over subjects, videos will be given that are the most relevant to beginners. If you need help, you can always join the voice chat or the live chat for study sessions with fellow peers. If that is not enough, tutors will also be monitoring the forums to provide the most and most accurate information just for you. Forum posts will be listed and some will be created by a tutor so keep an eye out for any study sessions!

Why Collab?

Collab was designed for a COVID-19 era. While many other video chat and tutoring software exist none of them streamline or have the same community feeling as Collab. Popular video chat software such as Discord, Skype, and Zoom all require personal invites, and or private rooms. Tutoring software such as Class + is more formal and requires students to make appointments beforehand.

Collab strives to combine both the functionality of popular video chat software along with the academic help of Class +. In order to achieve this our first goal was to recreate the feeling of impromptu study meetings with friends. Coffee shop meetups, library all nighters, dorm sleepovers, are all things absent in our current pandemic. In order to recreate this feeling we designed our course pages around being able to easily group up and chat. Be that through chat or video.

In today's world socialization is more important than ever, Collab enables you to succeed in your classes while simultaneously bringing you closer with those around you.
